Clean & Earn Referral Program

Rewarding you for spreading the sparkle ✨

🏁 Stage 1: Initial Launch (Brand Awareness)

🎁 Incentive: Both Referrer and Referral Get 10% Off

  • Referrer: Gets 10% off their next cleaning (1-time per referral).
  • Referral: Gets 10% off their first service.

Tactics:

  • Upsell the referral’s first visit to a premium deep clean with the 10% discount.
  • Offer easy online scheduling with a referral code field.

🚀 Stage 2: Growth Flywheel (Repeat Referrals)

🎁 Incentive: Tiered Rewards for Referrer

  • 1 referral: 10% off next clean
  • 3 referrals: One free add-on (e.g., oven, fridge, windows)
  • 5 referrals: 1 free standard clean
  • 10 referrals: 1 month of bi-weekly cleaning at 50% off

Tactics:

  • Free rewards are time-bound (must be redeemed in 30 days).
  • Add-on services increase value but are lower cost to you.
  • Encourage referrals to refer others (referral loops).

💎 Stage 3: VIP Loyalty Tier (Sustained Growth)

🎁 Incentive: “Clean Circle” Members (Top Referrers)

  • 15+ referrals = Lifetime 15% off
  • Priority holiday scheduling
  • Recognition on your site/socials

Tactics: Leverage their loyalty and social reach.

📲 Implementation

  • Referral Tracking: Unique code/link sent via email/SMS, QR cards after cleans.
  • Messaging: “Help your friends get a sparkling home—and save money.”
  • Creative: Leave-behind cards, social templates, referral contests.

📈 Metrics to Monitor

Metric What it Tells You
Referral conversion rate Quality of referrals
Average revenue per referred customer Long-term value
Referral channel success Optimize outreach method
Churn rate post-reward Customer retention value

💡 Revenue-Boosting Tactics

  • Time rewards during slow seasons
  • Require full-price booking before discount
  • Offer upgrades instead of cash discounts
